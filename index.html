Economic Indicators in Real Time

Economic Indicators are macroeconomic statistics used to understand the past, present, and future of an economy and its activity.

Macro economic actions and activity affect households' day to day expenses, savings, and decisions. Households drive demand, provide labor, save and invest, pay taxes, and are key players in an economy. Here, we track the key economic indicators across the G7 countries — Canada, France, Germany, Italy, Japan, the UK, and the US — that have a significant and direct affect on households and their financial well being.
