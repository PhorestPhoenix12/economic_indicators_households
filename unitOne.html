Labor Force Participation Rate: Frequency [Monthly]

This graph shows the share of the working-age population that is in the labor force. It helps illustrate the scarcity of labor and the opportunity cost of choosing between work and leisure.

Retail Gasoline Prices: Frequency [Weekly]

Gas prices fluctuate based on supply and demand. This graph shows how market forces and scarcity interact to determine price, making it a real-world example of equilibrium in competitive markets.

All Nonfarm Employees: Frequency [Monthly]

This graph tracks the number of people employed in nonfarm payroll jobs, covering over 80% of U.S. employment. It helps illustrate how labor is allocated across sectors, reflecting economic expansion or contraction and resource utilization over time.
